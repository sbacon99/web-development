About

Have you ever wanted to run away and join the circus? Do you like your exercise intense, or possibly in tents? Three-Ring Fitness (3RF) offers clients the circus experience with such exercise classes as contortion, trapeze, lyra, aerial silks, and tumbling. It's an awesome workout that feels more like playtime than exercise. People of all ages, sizes, and fitness levels need to dance, climb, play, and explore. At 3RF, we do just that! Join us to build strength and flexibility in a fun, creative environment.
