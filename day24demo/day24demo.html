About Me

I am currently an undergraduate Honors Fellow at Elon University, where I am pursuing degrees in Computer Science and Statistics with a concentration in data analytics. I am also a captain of the Elon men’s soccer team, an academic tutor, and a representative on Elon’s Student-Athlete Advisory Committee.

Over the last few years, I have developed a passion for data gathering and analysis, working with different computer software, and the variety of ways in which the two categories overlap. These interests have sparked my current research, in which I am analyzing and creating visualization tools to help assess the normality of various datasets and distributions.

My involvement in athletics has helped shape me into a committed, hard-working, and goal-oriented individual. As a college student-athlete, I have already experienced the transferability of soft skills such as communication, teamwork, and leadership. I am excited to continue developing as a student, researcher, and athlete, and I am eager to apply what I have learned through these passions in the professional world of data science and analytics.
